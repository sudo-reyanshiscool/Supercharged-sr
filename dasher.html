Body inspired by the peregrine falcon. Front wing inspired by the Rafale's canard. CO₂ chamber aligned to within 5 mm of the centre of mass — because rocket engineers don't accept tipping moments and neither do we.

Build Process · Six Steps

Step 01
Regulation block

Imported the official model block in Fusion 360. Locked dimensions with parametric constraints.

Step 02
Initial design

Splines and conic curves carved aerodynamic profiles into a half-block. Surface analysis kept airflow continuous.

Step 03
Mirror & offset

Mirrored the half body, controlled offsets slimmed regions for airflow, surface analysis confirmed alignment.

Step 04
CO₂ chamber

Revolve cut to regulation, chamber linked to global parameters for instant tolerance adjustments.

Step 05
CNC & SLS

CNC machined at 1 mm stepover, 2300 RPM. SLS 3D printed wings and fittings. Hybrid wins.

Step 06
Sand & paint

220 grit → 400 → 1000 grit sanding. Layered paint with grit pass between coats. Mass kept to 49 g.
