01
Doctrine · Why we build

A garage is a thesis statement

SUPERCHARGED is six engineers who refuse to round numbers up. We treat every gram, every micron and every millisecond as an open question — and the answer is always to build it again, lighter, sharper, faster.

We design in CAD, validate in CFD, machine on a 3-axis CNC, and prove the work on the 22.4 metre track at 1.04 seconds wide open. Then we tear it down and start over.

Method

CFD-driven aero. CNC and SLA hybrid manufacture. Vibration-damped axle assembly. Full telemetry on every test pass.

Discipline

Three test windows weekly. Drag coefficient logged per iteration. Nothing leaves the bench without a measured improvement over the last car.

Pedigree

Regional champion → national finalist → World Finals selection 2026. 17,000+ teams enter the STEM Racing pipeline globally. We're now in the top 24.
