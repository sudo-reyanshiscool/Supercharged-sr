Why this team matters

Five students. One idea too serious
to stay small.

We are still students, but we are building like the World Finals are real — because they are. Eighteen months in, our charter still fits on one line: be the first Indian team to win the Aramco STEM Racing World Finals.

Below is the team behind the car. Five names, two sub-teams, one livery. We led the build, the brand and the books ourselves — and the only adults in the room were the ones we asked for help.
