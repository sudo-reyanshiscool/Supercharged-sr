We're open to sponsorship enquiries, school visits, press, podcasts and STEM-outreach collaborations. Whether you're a brand looking for ROI on a young, fast-moving audience or a school wanting STEM Racing on your timetable — write to us. We answer in IST and we answer fast.

For Sponsors

Diamond, Gold and Silver tiers. Each comes with a brand visibility matrix, ROI report cadence, and a dedicated point of contact on the team.

For Press & Podcasts

We're available for podcast and press appearances. Send your brief and a date window to press@superchargedf1.com.

For Schools & Students

We give talks at schools across Delhi NCR and beyond. We've reached 800 + students through outreach so far. Book us via hello@superchargedf1.com.
